## Diesel Dewatering Pump Sound Attenuation Enclosures 🔥 (Active)
- Current project at STS/BCP
- **Science of sound attenuation** applied to industrial enclosures for portable diesel-powered dewatering pumps
- **Ventilation:** Calculating free net area, air speed in FPM
- **Acoustic treatment:** Multilayer wall lining system:
  - 2" open cell foam with aluminized protective layer
  - 2 lb/ft³ MLV (mass-loaded vinyl)
  - 1/4" foam insulation acting as decoupler
  - Adhesive layer contacting 11-gauge steel panels
- **Structural design:** Sheet metal bent to create tortuous sound path between structural pieces
- **Sealing:** Door sealing design, weather stripping geometry and material selection
- **Vibration management:** Isolators decoupling enclosure from chassis, chassis from engine; liquid vibration dampening compounds — where helpful, where not
- **Active learning:** Pursuing knowledge in acoustics, material science for sound control
