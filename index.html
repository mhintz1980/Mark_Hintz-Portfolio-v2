About

Mechanical designer and modern toolmaker with experience in diesel-powered industrial pump systems, sound-attenuated enclosures, firearms and related accessories, and the design/development of planetary reduction gearboxes that power industrial tooling. I work at the intersection of hardware and software: designing real-world equipment in SolidWorks, supporting machine shops, fabrication, and assembly—while building AI-powered tools that reduce friction in engineering, documentation, and troubleshooting.

I'm especially interested in roles that value practical, field-ready design plus smarter internal tools and workflows.
